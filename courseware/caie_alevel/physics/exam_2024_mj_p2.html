Question 1 (7分)

主题：运动学 Kinematics

(a) Define velocity and explain the difference between speed and velocity. [2]

(b) A car accelerates uniformly from rest to 24 m s⁻¹ in 8.0 seconds. It then continues at constant velocity for 12 seconds before decelerating uniformly to rest in 6.0 seconds.

(i) Calculate the acceleration during the first 8 seconds. [2]

(ii) Calculate the total distance travelled by the car. [3]

📝 详细解答

(a)

Velocity is the rate of change of displacement with respect to time.

Speed is a scalar quantity (magnitude only), velocity is a vector quantity (magnitude and direction).

(b)(i)

a = (v - u)/t = (24 - 0)/8.0 = 3.0 m s⁻²

(b)(ii)

First phase: s₁ = ½at² = 0.5 × 3.0 × 8² = 96 m

Second phase: s₂ = vt = 24 × 12 = 288 m

Third phase: s₃ = average velocity × time = 12 × 6 = 72 m

Total distance = 96 + 288 + 72 = 456 m

Question 2 (8分)

主题：动力学 Dynamics

(a) State Newton's second law of motion. [2]

(b) A mass of 5.0 kg is pulled along a horizontal surface by a horizontal force of 20 N. The frictional force opposing the motion is 8 N.

(i) Calculate the acceleration of the mass. [3]

(ii) Calculate the distance travelled in 4.0 seconds starting from rest. [3]

📝 详细解答

(a)

Newton's second law states that the rate of change of momentum of a body is directly proportional to the resultant force acting on it, and occurs in the direction of the force.

F = ma (for constant mass)

(b)(i)

Resultant force F = 20 - 8 = 12 N

F = ma → a = F/m = 12/5.0 = 2.4 m s⁻²

(b)(ii)

s = ut + ½at² = 0 + 0.5 × 2.4 × 4.0² = 19.2 m

Question 3 (7分)

主题：力 Forces

(a) Explain what is meant by the moment of a force about a point. [2]

(b) A uniform beam of length 2.0 m and weight 100 N is supported at its ends. A weight of 50 N is placed 0.5 m from one end.

(i) Calculate the reaction force at each support. [3]

(ii) State the two conditions for equilibrium. [2]

📝 详细解答

(a)

The moment of a force is the turning effect of the force about a point.

Moment = force × perpendicular distance from the point to the line of action of the force.

(b)(i)

Taking moments about left end: R₂ × 2.0 = 100 × 1.0 + 50 × 0.5 = 125

R₂ = 62.5 N

R₁ + R₂ = 150 → R₁ = 87.5 N

(b)(ii)

1. Resultant force in any direction is zero.

2. Resultant moment about any point is zero.
