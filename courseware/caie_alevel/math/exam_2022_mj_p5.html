Question 1 (10分)

主题：排列组合 Permutations & Combinations

(a) Find the number of ways to arrange the letters of the word "MATHEMATICS". [4]

(b) A committee of 5 is to be chosen from 6 boys and 4 girls. Find the number of ways this can be done if:

(i) there are no restrictions [2]

(ii) the committee must contain at least 2 boys and at least 2 girls [4]

📝 详细解答

(a)

Total letters = 11

Repeated letters: M appears 2 times, A appears 2 times, T appears 2 times

Other letters appear once.

Number of arrangements = 11! / (2! × 2! × 2!) = 39916800 / 8 = 4989600

(b)(i)

Total people = 10, choose 5

Number of ways = C(10, 5) = 252

(b)(ii)

Possible combinations:

Case 1: 2 boys and 3 girls: C(6,2) × C(4,3) = 15 × 4 = 60

Case 2: 3 boys and 2 girls: C(6,3) × C(4,2) = 20 × 6 = 120

Total = 60 + 120 = 180

Question 2 (10分)

主题：概率 Probability

(a) Two fair dice are thrown. Find the probability that:

(i) the sum is 7 [2]

(ii) at least one six is obtained [2]

(iii) the sum is 7 given that at least one six is obtained [3]

(b) Events A and B are such that P(A) = 0.6, P(B) = 0.3, P(A|B) = 0.5. Find:

(i) P(A ∩ B) [1]

(ii) P(A ∪ B) [2]

📝 详细解答

(a)(i)

Possible outcomes: (1,6), (2,5), (3,4), (4,3), (5,2), (6,1) → 6 outcomes

Total outcomes = 36

P(sum = 7) = 6/36 = 1/6

(a)(ii)

P(at least one six) = 1 - P(no sixes) = 1 - (5/6 × 5/6) = 1 - 25/36 = 11/36

(a)(iii)

P(sum = 7 | at least one six) = P(sum = 7 AND at least one six) / P(at least one six)

Sum = 7 AND at least one six: outcomes are (1,6) and (6,1) → 2 outcomes

Probability = 2/36

Conditional probability = (2/36) / (11/36) = 2/11

(b)(i)

P(A ∩ B) = P(A|B) × P(B) = 0.5 × 0.3 = 0.15

(b)(ii)

P(A ∪ B) = P(A) + P(B) - P(A ∩ B) = 0.6 + 0.3 - 0.15 = 0.75

Question 3 (12分)

主题：正态分布 Normal Distribution

(a) The heights of adult males are normally distributed with mean 175 cm and standard deviation 10 cm.

(i) Find the probability that a randomly chosen male is taller than 185 cm. [3]

(ii) Find the probability that a randomly chosen male is between 165 cm and 185 cm tall. [3]

(iii) 20% of males are taller than height h. Find h. [4]

(b) Explain why the normal distribution might not be a good model for family income. [2]

📝 详细解答

(a)(i)

X ~ N(175, 10²)

P(X > 185) = P(Z > (185-175)/10) = P(Z > 1) = 1 - Φ(1) = 1 - 0.8413 = 0.1587

(a)(ii)

P(165 < X < 185) = P(-1 < Z < 1) = Φ(1) - Φ(-1) = 0.8413 - 0.1587 = 0.6826

(a)(iii)

P(X > h) = 0.2 → P(X < h) = 0.8

Z value for 0.8 probability = 0.8416 (using inverse normal)

h = μ + zσ = 175 + 0.8416 × 10 = 183.42 cm

(b)

Normal distribution is symmetric, but income distribution is positively skewed (long tail to the right).

Most people have moderate incomes, few have very high incomes.

Income cannot be negative, but normal distribution allows negative values (though small probability).
