During the five-month dry season, when Bangladeshis typically farm very little, Abdul used the treadle pump to grow a quarter-acre of chili peppers, tomatoes, cabbage and eggplants. He also improved the yield of one of his rice plots by irrigating it. His family ate some of the vegetables and sold the rest at the village market, earning a net profit of $100. With his new income, Abdul was able to buy rice for his family to eat, keep his two sons in school until they were 16 and set aside a little money for his daughter’s dowry. 💡 解题技巧 Tips & Strategies

  1. Skimming（略读）：先快速浏览全文，掌握文章大意和结构，注意每段的首句和尾句
  2. Scanning（扫读）：根据题干中的关键词（人名、地名、数字等）回原文定位答案
  3. 同义替换：雅思阅读90%的答案都是原文内容的同义改写，注意积累常见替换词
  4. 时间管理：建议每篇文章用时不超过20分钟，难题先跳过，先做简单题
  5. 拼写准确：注意答案的拼写和语法形式，单复数、时态错误都会导致失分
