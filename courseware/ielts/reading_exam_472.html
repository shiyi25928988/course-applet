The article based on two studies was finally published in Harvard Business Review. Most authors’ arguments have laid emphasis on four-drive theory and actual investigations. Using the results of the surveys which executed with employees from Fortune 500 companies and other two global businesses (P company and H company), the article mentions about how independent drives influence employees’ behavior and how organizational levers boost employee motivation. The studies show that the drive to bond is most related to fulfilling commitment, while the drive to comprehend is most related to how much effort employees spend on works. The drive to acquire can be satisfied by a rewarding system which ties rewards to performances, and gives the best people opportunities for advancement. For drive to defend, a study on the merging of P company and H company shows that employees in former company show an unusual cooperating attitude. The key to successfully motivate employees is to meet all drives. Each of these drives is important if we are to understand employee motivation. These four drives, while not necessarily the only human drives, are the ones that are central to unified understanding of modern human life. 2

💡 解题技巧 Tips & Strategies

  1. Skimming（略读）：先快速浏览全文，掌握文章大意和结构，注意每段的首句和尾句
  2. Scanning（扫读）：根据题干中的关键词（人名、地名、数字等）回原文定位答案
  3. 同义替换：雅思阅读90%的答案都是原文内容的同义改写，注意积累常见替换词
  4. 时间管理：建议每篇文章用时不超过20分钟，难题先跳过，先做简单题
  5. 拼写准确：注意答案的拼写和语法形式，单复数、时态错误都会导致失分
