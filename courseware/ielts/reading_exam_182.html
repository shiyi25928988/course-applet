Tips: Yes/No/Nof Given Task guide    This task requires you to understand views expressed or claims made by the writer of the text.    To answer each question, you will need to ask yourself three questions:   (a)    Is the same view expressed in the text? (c)    Is there no view on this particular matter in the text?    For an answer to be 'No', the writer must directly state something that makes the statement in the question incorrect.    Questions to which the answer is 'Not Given' involve the writer saying something related to the statement in the question, but not expressing a view or making a claim on the specific point mentioned in the question.    This task requires you to look very closely at what the writer does say, with regard to each of the questions.    The questions follow the order in which the relevant issues are discussed or points mentioned in the text. 💡 解题技巧 Tips & Strategies

  1. Skimming（略读）：先快速浏览全文，掌握文章大意和结构，注意每段的首句和尾句
  2. Scanning（扫读）：根据题干中的关键词（人名、地名、数字等）回原文定位答案
  3. 同义替换：雅思阅读90%的答案都是原文内容的同义改写，注意积累常见替换词
  4. 时间管理：建议每篇文章用时不超过20分钟，难题先跳过，先做简单题
  5. 拼写准确：注意答案的拼写和语法形式，单复数、时态错误都会导致失分
